Z80 MBC2


This is the Z80 MBC2 I built a few years ago. it features MicroSD card support allowing it to use many gigabytes of ROM.
It additionally features 128K of banked RAM, and uses CP/M 3.0, CP/M 2.0 and MS BASIC as its operating systems. Despite
it being a very powerful system, especially compared to my pocket computer and Altoids computer, I never actually programmed
on it very much at all. Using assembly language on the system is all but completely undocumented, leading me to lose interest
in attempting to do it. Still, it was very fun to solder together and works perfectly even years after it was built!
