About Me

Hello! My name is David Lowry. I have been working with computers for almost a decade. I started at the age of 10 years old with Python 3. After trying (And failing) to program computer games using Python, I moved on to learning as many programming languages I could. This resulted in me having familiarity with C, C#, C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Z80 Assembly Language, x86 Assembly Language, Verilog, Java and BASIC. (Mainly Sinclair BASIC)

While I only retained a few of these, I continued to follow my passion of working with computers as time went on. I went to OSU for a while to pursue a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering, however, after a year I switched my major to Software Engineering and here I am today! I will continue to pursue my Bachelor's degree on a part time basis once I am employed, and look forward to sharing my works with you through this page.
